## Who We Are

Soraxis is the hardware and autonomous systems subsidiary of [Sorabbyngo](https://github.com/sorabbyngo-headquarters) — Africa's sovereign technology company.

We sit at the intersection of operating systems, AI, robotics, and space. Every device Soraxis builds runs on [Sorabbyngo OS](https://github.com/sorabbyngo-headquarters/sorabbyngo-os) — our sovereign, AI-native operating system — creating a vertically integrated ecosystem where hardware, software, intelligence, and connectivity are designed as one.

Think Tesla for autonomy. SpaceX for space infrastructure. NVIDIA for AI compute. Apple for integrated ecosystems. Built in Africa, built for the world.

## Why Soraxis

Africa has 1.4 billion people, vast natural resources, and rapidly growing infrastructure needs. Yet almost every device, robot, and sensor deployed on the continent runs foreign software, sends data to foreign servers, and depends on foreign supply chains.

Soraxis changes that. Every device we build:

- 🔒 **Runs sovereign software** — Sorabbyngo OS, built in Africa
- 📡 **Works offline** — full intelligence with zero connectivity
- 🆔 **Has hardware identity** — NodeID cryptographic device fingerprint
- 🌍 **Is built for Africa's realities** — dust, heat, low bandwidth, distributed deployment
- 🤝 **Interoperates natively** — every Soraxis device speaks the same protocol
